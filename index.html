About this Website

I built this website because I wanted a way to bulk download General Conference and BYU talks from apostles and prophets. I love listening to these talks on my walks and when doing other things, but it was annoying to have to click through every single one, download them individually, and then sort them all. My dad came up with the idea of using a program to automate it, and when I needed a project for work to practice my programming skills, this seemed like the perfect fit. Now, you can easily pick either BYU talks or General Conference talks or both, for the General Conference talks it will automatically download all of them and for the BYU talks it will redirect you to the speakers page where you can easily download all of them.
